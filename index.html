We ≠ Us

  • Breaking up is hard to do

    Thankfully, We ≠ Us is here to help by softening the blow of your next breakup text. Simply type your intended text into our system & click “Test”. We analyze it and return text a rating. When the rating is positive your text is good to go.

    If you are not sure where to start click on the ‘Help’ button for some suggestions.

  • How does it work?

    We use the AlchemyAPI (powered by Internet magic) to analyses content, context & emojis in order to calculate the tone of your message.
